Register to Vote
Use this service to:
  • Apply to registerto vote
  • Request for replacement of voter's card
  • Update your name, address or other details (e.g change of address, transfer card to another polling center etc.) on the electoral register
What you may need:
  • Biometric Id Number From Previously Captured data for Services like National Identity Number
  • Biometric Verification Number
  • International Passport Number
Other Means to Register to Vote
New Voter Registration (With Biometric Data)

Use this service - link to register for voter ID, if this is your first time registering to vote and if you have provided (captured) biometric data for any of this services (e.g National Identity Numbers, Drivers License, International Passport, & Bank Verification Number). By Using this service link, you will only be required to schedule visit to the Local Government Area Office Where you reside for collection of Card

New Voter Registration (Without Biometric Data)

Use this service - link to register for voter ID, if you have not provided (captured) biometric data for any of this services (e.g National Identity Numbers, Drivers License, International Passport, & Bank Verification Number, or Voter Id Card) before. You are required to visit LGA Office for a biometric Capture

Change your Voter data (e.g address,)

Use this service - link if you want to transfer your voting ID to another poling center To use this service, you must have your Biometric ID Captured for any of this services: National Identity Numbers, Driver License, International Passport, Bank Verification Number, or Voter Id Card.

Replace Voter Card

Use this service - link if you lost your Voter Card and would like to replace it. To use this service, you must have your biometric ID for any of this Services; National Identity Numbers, Driver License, International Passport, Bank Varification Number, or Voter ID Card.
